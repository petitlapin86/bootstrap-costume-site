About the Costume Designers Conference

The beautiful city of New Orleans, Louisiana will be the host city for this years conference!

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international design community and share skills with some of the world's top experts, thinkers, and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of Costume.

Cut Cloth is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

Networking: Meet film makers and industry professionals Technical: Tips from the best in the business Inspirational: Hear stories of success and experiences on set Union: Learn the steps to apply and the benefits Showcase: Portfolio critique

Speakers

Janie Bryant

Janie is best known for her work on AMC's MAD MEN - shes an expert on post WWI American fashion

Sandy Powell

Sandy is a powerhouse; she has contributed to the design of seven films that were nominated for the Best Picture Oscar. She has designed the gamut from Baz Luhrmann's Cinderella to the Aviator.

Colleen Atwood

Holds 4 Academy Awards and 7 nominations for her work in Memoirs of a Geisha, Chicago and Sweeney Todd to name a few.

Anna Robbins

Anna Robbins joined 'Downton Abbey' in season 5. Recently, she curated all the show's looks into a traveling exhibition.

Natalia Stewart

Breathes new life into historic designs at the Royal Opera House in London.

Margaret De Laroe

Last years "Young designer to watch" winner, has since worked on three new films.

About The Costume Designers Guild

The Costume Designers Guild (CDG) is Local 892 of the International Alliance of Theatrical and Stage Employees (I.A.T.S.E.). The Guild represents Costume Designers, Assistant Costume Designers and Costume Illustrators working at the highest levels of skill and expertise in motion pictures, television and commercials. The CDG promotes and protects the economic status of its members while improving working conditions and raising standards for our craft.
